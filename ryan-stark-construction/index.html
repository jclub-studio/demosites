Your Bitterroot Valley Construction Partner

Based in Victor, MT, Ryan Stark Construction brings over 15 years of experience building quality homes and structures throughout the Bitterroot Valley. We understand the unique challenges of mountain living and specialize in creating spaces that embrace Montana's natural beauty while withstanding its demanding climate.

From custom mountain homes to agricultural facilities, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern building techniques to deliver exceptional results. Our commitment to quality, fair pricing, and personal service has made us the trusted choice for homeowners, farmers, and businesses throughout Ravalli County.
