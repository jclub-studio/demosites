About Thayne

For over 40 years, Thayne has been the heart of hair care in Lolo, Montana. What started as a small barbershop has grown into a beloved community institution where generations of families come for master-level craftsmanship and genuine personal care.

Known as "The Child Whisperer," Thayne has a special gift for making children comfortable during their haircuts. Parents often share stories of how their children, who once feared haircuts, now look forward to their visits.

With a commitment to excellence and honest pricing, Thayne continues to serve the Lolo community with the same dedication that has earned him 4.9 stars from 66+ satisfied customers.
