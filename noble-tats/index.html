Frequently Asked Questions

Pricing varies based on size, complexity, and placement. During your consultation, we'll provide an estimate based on your specific design. As a general guideline, small tattoos start at $80, medium pieces range $150-300, and larger custom work is priced by the hour at $150/hour.

We recommend:

  • Get a good night's sleep before your appointment
  • Eat a substantial meal 1-2 hours before
  • Stay hydrated in the days leading up
  • Wear comfortable clothing that allows access to the tattoo area
  • Avoid alcohol 24 hours before
  • Bring any reference materials you have

We require 48 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Last-minute cancellations may require a non-refundable deposit for future bookings. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you in those situations.

We provide detailed aftercare instructions with each tattoo, but general guidelines include:

  • Keep the bandage on for 2-4 hours
  • Wash gently with mild, fragrance-free soap
  • Apply a thin layer of recommended ointment
  • Avoid swimming, soaking, or direct sunlight for 2 weeks
  • Don't pick at scabs
  • Keep moisturized during healing

We offer free touch-ups within 3 months if needed.

We primarily work by appointment to ensure each client receives the time and attention their tattoo deserves. However, we occasionally accept walk-ins when availability permits. It's always best to call ahead to check same-day availability.
