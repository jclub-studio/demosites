Infinity Construction and Repair, LLC is a locally owned general contracting company based in Superior, Montana. Since 2020, our family-operated business has built a solid reputation serving the greater Missoula area and surrounding communities.

We take great pride in providing exceptional workmanship and complete customer satisfaction from start to completion. Owner Jeremy Evans personally manages every project, ensuring attention to detail and quality that exceeds expectations.
