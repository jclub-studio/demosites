About M3 Construction

Founded by Fred Medlar, M3 Construction LLC brings over a decade of specialized construction experience to the Bitterroot Valley. As an owner-operated business, Fred personally oversees every project, ensuring quality workmanship and clear communication throughout.

We're fully licensed and insured, specializing in exterior construction, framing, and unique concrete core drilling services. Our commitment to being "fast, dependable, and fair" has made us a neighborhood favorite on Nextdoor.
