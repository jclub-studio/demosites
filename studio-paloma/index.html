About Studio Paloma

Founded in 2020, Studio Paloma is more than a beauty collective — we're a sanctuary where artistry meets transformation. Our name, inspired by the Spanish word for "dove," symbolizes peace, renewal, and the gentle beauty we help create.

Nestled in the heart of Richland, Washington, our studio brings together master artists specializing in permanent makeup, areola restoration, tiny tattoos, and luxury beauty services. Each artist brings their unique expertise, creating a collaborative environment where innovation thrives.

We believe beauty is deeply personal. Every service is tailored to enhance your natural features, boost confidence, and celebrate your individual story. From delicate microblading to compassionate areola restoration, we approach each client with expertise, empathy, and artistic vision.
