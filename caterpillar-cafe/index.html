Our Story

Since 2015, Caterpillar Cafe has been serving exceptional coffee and tea in a unique literary-themed environment. Connected to Adventures Underground bookstore, we're a community-focused cafe that celebrates books, pop culture, and local creativity.

Our Philosophy

"Think globally, act locally" drives everything we do. We support local arts and community through donations and events, creating a space where people feel "seen" and welcomed.

Our cafe is a little bit geeky, a little bit rock 'n roll. We're enthusiastic about coffee, tea, beer, music, books, movies, and superheroes. Whether you're here for a literary-themed drink or to play Dungeons & Dragons, you'll find a welcoming community.
