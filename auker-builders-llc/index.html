About Auker Builders

Family-owned and operated since 2017, delivering excellence in construction, excavation, and land management across rural Montana.

Our Story

Founded in 2017 by James Auker, Auker Builders LLC has grown from a local construction company to a comprehensive contractor serving both private clients and government agencies across northwestern Montana.

With deep roots in the Alberton community and extensive federal contracting experience, we bring together local expertise with government-grade standards to deliver exceptional results for every project.
