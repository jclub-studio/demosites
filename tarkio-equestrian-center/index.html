Our Facilities

Indoor Riding Arena

Our climate-controlled indoor arena allows for year-round riding regardless of Montana's weather conditions. Features excellent footing, professional lighting, and viewing areas.

Outdoor Facilities

Multiple outdoor arenas, round pens, and extensive trail access to the surrounding Lolo National Forest provide diverse riding experiences.

Boarding Amenities

Spacious box stalls, run-in sheds, and lush pastures ensure your horse's comfort and well-being. Our facility includes modern tack rooms, wash areas, and feed storage.

Trail Access

Direct access to miles of scenic trails through the Bitterroot Range mountains, offering some of Montana's most breathtaking riding experiences.

About Tarkio Equestrian Center

Our Story

Located in the heart of Superior, Montana, Tarkio Equestrian Center combines professional horsemanship with the natural beauty of Mineral County. Our experienced team is dedicated to providing exceptional care for your horses and quality instruction for riders of all ages and skill levels.
