Our Story

Founded in 1985, Templer Knight Construction has been an integral part of Ronan's growth and development. From our humble beginnings as a small family operation, we've grown to become Lake County's premier general contractor while maintaining our commitment to quality and community.

Our deep understanding of Montana's unique climate challenges, combined with our respectful approach to working within the Flathead Indian Reservation, sets us apart from other contractors in the region.
