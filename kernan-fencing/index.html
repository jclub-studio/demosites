About Kernan Fencing Company

Kernan Fencing Company, owned by Nathan Kernan, has been providing top-quality fencing solutions in Prosser, WA and surrounding areas. With a BuildZoom score of 94, placing us in the top 24% of Washington licensed contractors, we pride ourselves on exceptional craftsmanship and customer satisfaction.

Our team is fully licensed, insured up to $300,000, and bonded to ensure your project is protected from start to finish. We specialize in a variety of fencing materials and services to meet all your residential and commercial needs.

With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, Kernan Fencing Company is your trusted partner for all fencing projects.
