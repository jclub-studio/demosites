Our Story

Welcome to The Forest Wedding Venue, nestled in the heart of Washington's renowned wine country near the charming town of Prosser. For over a decade, we've been crafting unforgettable wedding experiences amidst our 26-acre forest estate.

We pride ourselves on being more than just a venue. As a premier cultural hub in the hospitality industry, our dedicated team of 3 professionals delivers exceptional service to approximately 120 couples each year, generating $1.6M in annual revenue while maintaining the intimate charm that sets us apart.
