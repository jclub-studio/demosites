About Tipsy Nail Bar

Founded in 2023, Tipsy Nail Bar has quickly become Kennewick's premier destination for exceptional nail care. Our family-owned salon is committed to "raising the bar in the nail industry" through superior service and a welcoming atmosphere.

What sets us apart is our unique combination of professional expertise, a fun beverage service, and unwavering commitment to hygiene. We believe that nail care should be an enjoyable, relaxing experience that leaves you feeling confident and beautiful.
