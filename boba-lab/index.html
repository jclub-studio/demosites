OUR LAB STORY

Since April 21, 2023, Boba Lab has been Kennewick's premier destination for innovative bubble tea experiences. As a family-owned establishment, we blend traditional techniques with modern creativity.

Our welcoming space features a modern grass wall backdrop, entertainment options including games and TV, and an atmosphere designed for both casual hangouts and special moments.
