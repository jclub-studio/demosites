Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Tate Stinnett, Stinnett Construction has been proudly serving the Plains, Montana community and surrounding Sanders County for nearly a decade.

As a family-owned and operated business, we understand the unique building challenges of rural Montana. From harsh winters to remote locations, we've built our reputation on delivering quality craftsmanship that stands the test of time and weather.
