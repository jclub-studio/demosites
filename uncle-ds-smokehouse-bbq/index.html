OUR STORY

Family Legacy Since 1920

Crafting Legendary BBQ for Generations

Founded by pitmaster Darryl, Uncle D's Smokehouse BBQ & Grill carries on the legacy of his great-grandmother and grandmother's homestyle backyard cooking traditions. Our California-style barbecue is rooted in the Coachella Valley community.

Using our proprietary "top secret" seasoning recipe perfected by owner "Big John" Glover, we slow-smoke our meats to perfection, creating tender, flavorful dishes that keep our customers coming back for more.
