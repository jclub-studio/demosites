About Heat On Meat

Bringing authentic BBQ to Florence, Missoula & Ravalli Counties

Our Story

Founded in Florence, Montana, Heat On Meat started as a passion for authentic BBQ and a desire to bring quality smoked meats to our local community. What began as a small food truck operation has grown into a beloved local institution serving Missoula and Ravalli Counties.

Our signature smoking process uses locally sourced meats and traditional techniques, resulting in tender, flavorful dishes that keep our customers coming back for more.

We pride ourselves on being a locally-owned business that's deeply rooted in our community. Whether it's a small family gathering or a large corporate event, we're committed to bringing the heat to your meat!
