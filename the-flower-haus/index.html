About The Flower Haus

Welcome to The Flower Haus, your local florist in Lolo, Montana. As a family-owned business, we take pride in providing personalized service and beautiful floral arrangements for every occasion.

Since our establishment, we've been serving the community with fresh flowers, creative designs, and exceptional customer service. Whether it's a wedding, corporate event, or everyday bouquet, we're here to make your moments special.
