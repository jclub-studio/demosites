Our Story

Family-Owned Forest Sanctuary

Nestled in 10 acres of pristine Pacific Northwest forest, The Forest Wedding Venue has been our family's passion project for over a decade. What began as a private retreat has blossomed into a magical setting where couples begin their forever.

We've carefully preserved the natural beauty while creating intimate spaces perfect for your special day. Every tree, clearing, and pathway tells a story - now we invite you to write yours here.

Frequently Asked Questions

All our packages include access to our beautiful forest ceremony sites, reception areas, basic tables and chairs, setup/cleanup crew, and on-site parking. The Intimate Wedding package also includes a bridal preparation cabin and groom's lounge. Additional items like linens, decor, and specialty lighting can be rented through our preferred vendors.

Yes! We have a beautiful covered pavilion that can accommodate ceremonies and receptions in case of rain. The pavilion has sides that can be lowered for wind protection while still maintaining views of the forest. We'll work with you as your date approaches to monitor the forecast and make the best decision for your special day.

We have a list of preferred vendors who know our property well and consistently deliver excellent service, but you're welcome to bring in your own vendors (with the exception of catering - all food must be provided by our approved caterers for insurance reasons). All vendors must be approved in advance and provide proof of insurance.

Prosser has several charming hotels and B&Bs just 15 minutes from our venue. We're happy to provide a list of recommendations with various price points. Additionally, we have two on-site cabins available for rent (perfect for the wedding party) and can arrange shuttle services from local hotels for your guests' convenience.

We allow beer, wine, and champagne served by our licensed caterers or a licensed bartender. Hard alcohol is permitted but must be served by a licensed bartender. All alcohol service must end one hour before your event concludes. We require a licensed security guard for events with 50+ guests where alcohol is served.
