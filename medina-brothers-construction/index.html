About Medina Brothers

Family-owned construction company serving Tri-Cities with excellence since 2007

Our Story

Established on May 20, 2007, Medina Brothers Construction LLC has been the trusted name in Tri-Cities construction for over 18 years. Led by Ariel Medina, Luis Medina Avalos, and principal Reynaldo Medina, we've built our reputation on quality, integrity, and exceptional customer service.

From our base in Kennewick, we've completed hundreds of residential projects, earning a BuildZoom score of 98 - placing us in the top 16% of all Washington contractors.
